# Next Chapter v2.5.0 — Enterprises, Auctions, and Special Careers

## Added

### Major Enterprise Expansion
- Culture museums, wildlife sanctuaries, and entertainment resorts
- Staff, reputation, appeal, security, specialist quality, annual revenue, expenses, profit, and value
- Upgrades, advertising, hiring, security reviews, specialty improvements, public events, inspections, and sales
- Enterprise assets contribute to net worth and inheritance

### Collections & Auctions
- A refreshed fictional auction market each year
- Five collectible categories with rarity and uncertain authenticity
- Appraisals, changing values, sales, and museum display bonuses
- Collections contribute to net worth and inheritance

### Special Careers
- Space explorer, model, actor, musician, athlete, public service, and intelligence tracks
- Applications, training, networking, work opportunities, ranks, salary, reputation, and awards
- Special-career annual updates in the Life Journal

### Developer controls
- Enterprises always profit
- All collectibles authentic
- Special-career actions always succeed
- Add all current auction lots
- Max or clear every v2.5 system

## Reference approach
The update uses original Next Chapter code, wording, data, and interface components. General life-simulator categories were studied as functional references; no proprietary application code, dialogue, or artwork is included.
