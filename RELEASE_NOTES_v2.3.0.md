# Next Chapter v2.3.0 — Living Headlines and Expansion Pack

## Life Journal updates

Age progression can now create compact journal headlines for family, investing, crypto, businesses, renting, landlords, social media, and fame.

Examples include:

- A sibling graduating kindergarten or school
- A relative earning a degree or beginning a career
- A company investment surging or falling
- A digital asset rallying or crashing
- A business reporting annual profit or loss
- A rental property producing income or requiring repairs
- A creator account gaining or losing followers

## Crypto Expansion

- Eight randomized fictional digital assets per life
- Fractional-unit buying and selling
- Cost basis, fees, holdings, realized results, and transaction history
- Large annual price swings and fictional market events
- Price-history line charts

## Business Expansion

- Eight business categories
- Up to eight owned businesses
- Employees, quality, advertising, reputation, revenue, expenses, profit, and value
- Invest, advertise, hire, or sell
- Annual business results and history charts

## Renting & Landlord Expansion

- Five personal rental-home options
- Deposits, yearly rent, active leases, and move-out refunds
- Five landlord property categories
- Tenants, vacancy, rent collection, maintenance, renovations, appreciation, and sales

## Social media and fame

- Followers, engagement, reputation, creator tiers, monetization, streaks, and creator income
- Photo stories, podcasts, fan community events, and premium sponsorships
- Public image, press mentions, awards, endorsements, and fan events
- Red carpets, fan meetings, merchandise, and documentaries
- Passive audience movement and fame decay during age progression

## Developer controls

- Force profitable crypto years
- Disable crypto fees
- Add all crypto holdings
- Force a crypto moonshot or crash
- Make businesses always profitable
- Keep landlord properties occupied
- Make social posts go viral
- Prevent fame decay
- Max or clear the new expansion systems

## Compatibility

Existing saves are migrated automatically when opened. Investment, crypto, businesses, and landlord assets contribute to net worth and can pass into later generations.
