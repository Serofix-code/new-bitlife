# Next Chapter v2.4.0 — Public Services, Surgery Clinic, and Vampire Expansion

## Country-based public services

Every supported country now carries a simplified game policy for:

- Healthcare coverage
- Child and guardian-paid care
- Orphanage healthcare costs
- Public-college tuition support

Children with a living parent or guardian do not personally pay for Health & Mind activities. Children in orphanage care use the current country's simplified coverage model instead. Adult doctor and therapy prices also change by country.

Higher-education tuition now changes by country. Some countries use a tuition-free public-college model, while others use low-fee, subsidized, high-fee, or full-fee models.

These are simplified game systems rather than legal, medical, or financial guidance.

## Surgery Clinic

The Style & Appearance center now opens a complete elective-care system for adult characters.

- Two randomly generated fictional surgeons are shown for each procedure
- Each surgeon has a price and reputation score
- Reputation, health, and procedure complexity affect success odds
- Procedures have individual lifetime limits and reassessment windows
- Successful procedures can increase the Looks game statistic and appearance satisfaction
- Complications can reduce Looks, happiness, and health
- Eligible complications can lead to a malpractice lawsuit
- A surgeon refuses future treatment after being sued
- Body procedures are unavailable during a pending pregnancy

Available procedures include Botox, lower-body contouring, chest augmentation, eyelid surgery, face lifts, liposuction, genital enhancement, gender-affirming surgery, nose surgery, and abdominal contouring.

Gender-affirming care can update the character's identity and pronouns, but the game explicitly treats identity as valid with or without medical treatment.

## Vampire Expansion overhaul

Vampire lives now include:

- An Aristocrat disguise and separate True Vampire form
- Lord or Lady noble titles
- Essence, secrecy, and notoriety statistics
- A randomized Maker and blood curse
- Three randomized Vampire Lord trials
- A permanent 100% stat-lock power
- Non-graphic night hunting with multiple approaches and locations
- Rival-vampire and vampire-hunter encounters
- Adult partner and child progeny
- Human familiars and loyalty choices
- Hypnosis to reduce suspicion and criminal heat
- Vampire Lairs and Vampire Castles
- Secure resting actions
- Vampire Lord rank and maximum notoriety after all trials are completed
- Visual aging suspended beyond ordinary human lifespans
- Biological family-building disabled after vampirism; adoption remains available
