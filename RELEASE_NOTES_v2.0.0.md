# Next Chapter v2.0.0 — Dynamic Activities

Version 2.0 expands the variable-driven design introduced by the fertility system across the Activities menu.

## Dynamic activity centres

- Health & Mind: doctor visits, gym, meditation, library, therapy, and diet plans
- Social Media: posts, creative videos, livestreams, collaborations, and brand campaigns
- Clubs & Teams: drama, sports, music, debate, and volunteering
- Licenses: driving, boating, and pilot tests
- Civil Law: consultations and several claim types
- Vacations: local, domestic, international, and luxury trips
- Movie Theater: multiple genres with different age requirements and effects
- Nightlife: cafés, concerts, dance venues, and fame-gated private events
- Fame Actions: interviews, memoirs, commercials, photoshoots, and charity appearances
- Pets: adoption approval, household capacity, pet health, age, and bond
- Style & Appearance: hairstyle, colour, wardrobe, accessories, and consultation options

Every centre displays changing metrics, estimated outcome chances, costs, restrictions, current progress, and recent history. Results are influenced by age, health, happiness, knowledge, resilience, fame, followers, talents, finances, career, housing, legal record, and past experience.

## Rules and state handling

- Jail continues to replace outside Activities completely.
- Age restrictions remain enforced unless the developer option disables them.
- Criminal records can reduce licensing, pet adoption, and civil-law outcomes.
- Owned licenses, memberships, pets, travel history, and activity histories persist in saves.
- Existing v1.9 saves migrate automatically.

## Developer tools

- Activities always succeed
- Unlimited activity points
- Max activity systems
- Clear dynamic activity histories
- Existing ribbon, challenge, money, fertility, occult, career, and justice controls remain available
