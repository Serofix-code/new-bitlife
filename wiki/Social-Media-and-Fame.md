# Social Media and Fame

## Social media

The Social Media system tracks:

- Followers
- Engagement
- Creator reputation
- Posting streak
- Creator level
- Monetization
- Lifetime creator income
- Largest follower gain from one post

Available activities include life updates, creative videos, photo stories, livestreams, podcasts, collaborations, fan community events, brand campaigns, and premium sponsorships.

Inactive accounts can lose momentum during age progression. Successful creator actions can improve fame and produce income.

## Fame

The Fame system tracks:

- Current fame
- Public image
- Press mentions
- Awards
- Endorsements
- Fan events

Actions include interviews, memoirs, commercials, photoshoots, charity appearances, red-carpet events, fan meetings, merchandise, and documentaries.

Fame can decay when a character stops making public appearances.
