# Investment Expansion

The Investment Expansion adds a fictional market with twelve randomly generated companies per life.

Each company has:

- A fictional name and ticker
- Sector
- Current and previous price
- Annual percentage change
- Risk level and volatility
- Dividend yield
- All-time high and low
- Price history
- Optional research report

Trading unlocks at age 18. Research unlocks at age 16 and consumes an activity point. Players can buy and sell whole shares. The portfolio tracks average cost, total cost, current value, unrealized gain or loss, realized gain or loss, dividends, and transaction history.

Prices change at every age-up. Market mood, sector drift, company volatility, and random fictional news affect the result. High-risk companies can experience severe setbacks. Dividend-paying companies may provide annual cash income.

All companies and results are fictional. This is a game mechanic and not financial advice.


Each company card now includes a recent line preview so players can quickly see whether the price has been trending up or down.
