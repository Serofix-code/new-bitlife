# Life Updates

The Life Journal combines major choice cards with smaller age-up headlines.

Compact updates can appear for:

- First steps and school milestones
- Kindergarten, school, and university graduations
- Relatives beginning careers or retiring
- Investment rallies and declines
- Crypto moonshots and crashes
- Business profits or losses
- Rent payments, landlord income, vacancy, or maintenance
- Social-media follower growth or decline
- Fame awards, merchandise, and documentary releases

Updates use the actual names and relationships stored in the current save. For example, a sibling milestone uses that sibling’s generated name rather than generic text.
