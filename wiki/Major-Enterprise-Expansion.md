# Major Enterprise Expansion

Adult characters can own and manage public-facing venues.

## Venue types

- Culture Museum
- Wildlife Sanctuary
- Entertainment Resort

## Management variables

Every venue tracks staff, reputation, appeal, security, specialist quality, annual revenue, costs, profit, and value. Owners can upgrade facilities, advertise, hire staff, review security, improve the venue specialty, host events, or sell.

Annual events can include inspections, popular exhibitions, conservation projects, entertainment events, and unexpected operating costs. Major enterprises contribute to net worth and inheritance.
