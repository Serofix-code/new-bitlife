# Developer Cheats

The Developer menu contains testing controls for age, stats, money, events, relationships, careers, crime, court, jail, occults, fertility, dynamic activities, account ribbons, challenges, and expansions.

## Investment cheats

- Add investment cash
- Add 100 shares of every company
- Force a 50% market boom
- Force a 50% market crash
- Make future market years profitable
- Disable trading fees
- Reveal company outlooks
- Max the investment expansion
- Clear the entire portfolio and history

Developer actions can heavily change game balance and are intended for testing and private customization.
