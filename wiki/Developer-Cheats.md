# Developer Cheats

The Developer menu contains testing controls for age, stats, money, events, relationships, careers, crime, court, jail, occults, fertility, dynamic activities, account ribbons, challenges, and expansions.

## Investment controls

- Add investment cash
- Add shares of every company
- Force a market boom or crash
- Make future market years profitable
- Disable trading fees
- Reveal company outlooks
- Max or clear the portfolio

## Crypto controls

- Add 10,000 units of every fictional crypto asset
- Force a 150% crypto moonshot
- Force an 80% crypto crash
- Make future crypto years profitable
- Disable crypto trading fees

## Business, landlord, and public-life controls

- Make every business profitable
- Keep landlord properties occupied
- Make social-media posts go viral
- Prevent fame decay
- Max every v2.3 expansion and public-life system
- Clear crypto, businesses, leases, and landlord properties

## Healthcare, surgery, and vampire controls

- Make every elective procedure succeed
- Make elective procedures free
- Give vampires unlimited essence
- Make vampire actions always succeed
- Complete the Vampire Lord trials
- Max all v2.4 systems
- Clear procedure and malpractice history

Developer actions can heavily change game balance and are intended for testing and customization.
