# Changelog

## v2.8.0

- Baby/toddler portraits for ages 0–4
- Child portraits for ages 5–12
- Teen portraits for ages 13–17
- Adult portraits from age 18 and senior styling later in life
- Identity-aware facial and clothing details for all characters
- Automatic portrait aging for players, NPCs, heirs, existing saves, and age-changing events

## v2.7.0

- Dated, randomized birth records and family-origin stories
- Adoption or blended-family histories for families with two mothers or two fathers
- Clickable Family Legacy with relatives, ancestors, and causes of death
- Richer cartoon portraits, additional hairstyles, and corrected live preview updates
- Age Up returns to the Life Journal and stats no longer cover subpages

## v2.6.0
- Refreshed the popup style used by event, save, menu, and expansion dialogs.
- Added an in-game Changelog menu option.
- Added a dedicated Changelog popup so players can read recent updates inside the app.
