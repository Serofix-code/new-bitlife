# Collections and Auctions

The fictional auction market refreshes every year. Lots can be artwork, historical curios, sports memorabilia, rare jewelry, or science collectibles.

## How it works

- Each lot has a rarity and asking price.
- Authenticity remains uncertain until appraisal.
- A reproduction loses much of its estimated value after appraisal.
- Values change during age progression.
- Items can be sold to private buyers.
- Collectibles can be displayed in an owned Culture Museum for an appeal bonus.

Collections contribute to net worth and inheritance.
