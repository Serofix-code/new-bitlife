# Business Expansion

The Business Expansion allows adult characters to start, grow, and sell companies.

## Business types

- Neighbourhood café
- Online store
- Creative studio
- Style salon
- Fitness centre
- Logistics company
- Technology startup
- Boutique hotel

A character can own up to eight businesses.

## Business variables

Each company tracks:

- Employees
- Quality
- Advertising
- Reputation
- Annual revenue
- Expenses and wages
- Annual profit or loss
- Current business value
- Historical results

Owners can invest in quality, advertise, hire employees, or sell the company. Profits add cash. Losses can reduce cash or create debt. Business value contributes to net worth and inheritance.
