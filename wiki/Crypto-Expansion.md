# Crypto Expansion

The Crypto Expansion adds eight randomly generated fictional digital assets to each life.

## Market behaviour

- Every asset has a name, symbol, price, risk level, volatility, and price history.
- Prices update whenever the character ages up.
- Crypto can move much more sharply than company shares.
- Fictional market events can produce confidence crises, rallies, crashes, or moonshots.
- Every asset includes a line-chart preview.

## Trading

Trading unlocks at age 18. Players can buy and sell fractional units. The game tracks:

- Units owned
- Average cost
- Total cost basis
- Current value
- Realized and unrealized results
- Trading fees
- Transaction history

Large annual movements can appear as compact Life Journal updates. The entire market is fictional.
