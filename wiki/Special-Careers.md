# Special Careers

Special Careers are deeper paths with applications, training, networking, ranks, annual salary, performance, reputation, opportunities, and awards.

## Available paths

- Space Explorer
- Professional Model
- Screen Performer
- Recording Artist
- Professional Athlete
- Public Service Career
- Intelligence Officer

Character stats and special talents affect application and opportunity success. Successful opportunities can pay money, raise fame, improve performance, and lead to higher ranks. Special careers do not pass to an heir, but the assets earned from them do.
