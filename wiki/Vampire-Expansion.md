# Vampire Expansion

The Vampire Expansion turns vampire lives into a deeper supernatural progression system.

## Dual forms

Every vampire has:

- An Aristocrat disguise used for ordinary public life
- A True Vampire form with separate visual settings

The player may switch the displayed form and randomize the True Vampire appearance.

## Core statistics

- **Essence:** supernatural energy required for powers
- **Secrecy:** protection from discovery
- **Notoriety:** influence within the supernatural world

Essence declines with age and certain curses. When it becomes very low, powers weaken and wellbeing suffers.

## Maker, curse, and trials

A Maker gives every vampire three randomized trials. Completing all three breaks the curse and grants the rank of Vampire Lord, maximum notoriety, and dominance powers.

## Powers

A vampire can lock one chosen game statistic at 100%:

- Health
- Happiness
- Smarts
- Resilience
- Looks

## Activities

- Hunt for essence in several fictional night locations
- Choose an ambush, friendship, or enticement approach
- Transform an adult spouse, partner, or child into progeny
- Recruit up to three human familiars
- Respond when familiars ask to be turned
- Hypnotize witnesses to reduce suspicion and criminal heat
- Buy a Vampire Lair or Vampire Castle
- Rest in owned vampire properties

All hunting and supernatural conflict is described in abstract, non-graphic terms.

## Restrictions

Vampirism is permanent during ordinary play. Biological family-building becomes unavailable, although adoption remains possible. Vampires do not die from ordinary aging at normal human ages, but they can still be harmed by events and low health.
