# Surgery and Gender-Affirming Care

The Surgery Clinic is found inside **Activities → Style & Appearance**.

## Requirements

- Character must be at least 18
- Character must have one activity point
- Character must be able to afford the selected fictional surgeon
- Body procedures are unavailable during a pending pregnancy
- Procedure limits and reassessment windows must be respected

## Surgeons

Every procedure displays two fictional surgeons. Each has:

- A reputation score
- A price
- A calculated success chance

Higher reputation improves the chance of a good result, but no surgeon guarantees success.

## Outcomes

A successful result can improve the Looks game statistic, happiness, and appearance satisfaction. Complications can reduce Looks, happiness, and health. Eligible complications may be taken to civil court as a malpractice claim.

Once a character sues a surgeon, that surgeon refuses future appointments.

## Gender-affirming care

Gender-affirming surgery is a one-time adult option that can update the profile identity and pronouns. Reproductive role remains a separate setting. The game treats identity as valid whether or not medical treatment is chosen.
