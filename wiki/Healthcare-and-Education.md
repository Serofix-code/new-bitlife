# Healthcare and Education

Next Chapter uses simplified country policies to make healthcare and higher education change with location.

## Healthcare

Health & Mind costs are calculated from:

- Character age
- Whether a living parent or guardian is present
- Whether the character is in orphanage care
- The country's simplified healthcare model
- Whether the activity is essential care, therapy, general wellness, or elective cosmetic care

A character under 18 with a living parent or guardian does not personally pay for Health & Mind activities. In orphanage care, country coverage applies without a family sponsor.

Elective cosmetic care is self-funded unless a developer cheat changes the price.

## College

Trade academy, city college, and graduate tuition use the current country's simplified tuition multiplier. Models include:

- Tuition-free public college
- Low public tuition
- Subsidized public college
- High tuition with public support
- Full tuition

Any annual tuition the character cannot afford becomes student debt through the normal education system.

The policy labels are game abstractions and are not legal or financial advice.
