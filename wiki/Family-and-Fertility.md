# Family and Fertility

## Birth stories and parentage

Every new life begins with a complete date of birth and a seeded, randomized family story. Families with two mothers or two fathers are represented through either adoption or a blended-family history in which one parent had the child before meeting the new parent. The parent labels shown in the Family Legacy identify biological, adoptive, and step-parent relationships.

Click the current character's name in the profile header to open the Family Legacy. It lists current relatives, deceased family members with causes of death, and playable ancestors from earlier generations.

Family-building unlocks at age 18. The Biological Success metric ranges from 0 to 100 and uses age, health, reproductive role, partner compatibility, contraception, occult modifiers, and the chosen route.

Available routes include natural attempts, artificial insemination, IVF, surrogacy, and adoption. Contraception applies a multiplier to natural attempts. Adoption checks adulthood, finances, stability, housing, and criminal history.

A successful route creates a pending process. The child arrives through a later age-up event instead of appearing instantly.
