# Renting and Landlords

The Renting & Landlord Expansion contains two connected systems.

## Renting a home

Adult characters can rent:

- A shared room
- A city studio
- A two-bed apartment
- A family house
- A luxury penthouse

A new lease requires a deposit and the first yearly rent payment. Rent is paid again during age progression. Ending the lease returns part of the deposit.

## Landlord properties

Characters can purchase:

- A small rental flat
- A city apartment
- A suburban family home
- A residential duplex
- A small apartment block

Properties track monthly rent, tenants, vacancies, maintenance, condition, appreciation, value, and yearly net income. Owners can find tenants, renovate, or sell.

Landlord property values contribute to net worth and can pass to a playable heir.
