# Next Chapter v2.1.0 — Investment Expansion and Wiki

## Investment Expansion

- Added an Expansions section to the game menu and Activities page.
- Added twelve randomly generated fictional companies per life.
- Added company sectors, tickers, price histories, risk, volatility, dividends, annual change, and market news.
- Trading unlocks at age 18; research unlocks at age 16.
- Added buying and selling with whole shares and fictional trading fees.
- Added portfolio value, average cost, realized results, unrealized results, dividends, and transaction history.
- Investment prices update every age-up.
- Prison blocks new trades while owned investments continue changing value.
- Investments contribute to net worth and can pass to the next generation.

## Developer tools

- Add investment cash.
- Add shares in every company.
- Force market booms and crashes.
- Force profitable future markets.
- Remove trading fees.
- Reveal company outlooks.
- Max or clear the Investment Expansion.

## Documentation

- Added `guide.html` to the public game and main menu.
- Added a complete Markdown game guide under `wiki/`.
- Documented character creation, progression, Activities, family systems, relationships, crime, prison, supernatural lives, investments, saving, challenges, ribbons, and developer controls.

## Compatibility

Existing saves migrate automatically to schema 12. A market is generated the first time an older life is loaded.
